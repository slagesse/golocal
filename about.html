About golocal.

Golocal was created as a way of sharing your own favorite fast and environmentally friendly routes around town with visitors or people unfamiliar with the area.

To get started, click on the Upload tab in the upper nav. Or, to browse other people's routes, click on the Browse

Golocal was created in under 36 hours for the 2022 TigerHacks hackathon, by Mizzou freshman Shane LaGesse.
